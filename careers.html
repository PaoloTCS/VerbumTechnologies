Application Process

We're looking for passionate individuals who share our vision of advancing AI through physics-inspired innovation. Our application process is designed to be straightforward and focused on your research interests and technical capabilities.

How to Apply

  1. Email Application: Send your CV/resume and a cover letter to paolo@verbumtechnologies.com
  2. Research Statement: Include a brief statement about your research interests and how they align with our work
  3. Portfolio: For technical positions, include links to relevant projects, publications, or code repositories
  4. Interview Process: Selected candidates will be invited for technical discussions and research presentations

Note: We're always interested in hearing from talented researchers, even if there isn't a specific position listed. Feel free to reach out with your research interests and background.
