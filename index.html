VerbumTechnologies

VerbumTechnologies is a research initiative developed under the umbrella of Litchfield Capital Corporation .

The central theme of VerbumTechnologies is the collaborative exploration of Artificial Intelligence and fundamental theory. Projects span linguistics, machine learning, physics, computation and the philosophy of computation. We seek to understand and develop new models of AI.

The engine of this initiative is a partnership between human inquiry and language models, working together as co-investigators.
