About Our Team

Our team brings together expertise from artificial intelligence, theoretical physics, computer science, and cognitive science. We believe that the most groundbreaking advances in AI will come from interdisciplinary collaboration and a deep understanding of fundamental principles.

We're passionate about building AI systems that are not just powerful, but also principled, interpretable, and aligned with the physical laws that govern our universe.
